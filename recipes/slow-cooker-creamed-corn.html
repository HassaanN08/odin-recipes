Slow Cooker Creamed Corn

Why You'll Love This Recipe

This stress-free side is made in the slow cooker with frozen corn and basic fridge staples.
Home cooks love the rich creaminess from cream cheese and butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ (16 ounce) packages frozen corn kernels
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine corn, cream cheese, butter, milk, and sugar in a slow cooker. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cook on Low for 4 to 6 hours or High for 2 to 4 hours, stirring once or twice during cooking to prevent sticking.
